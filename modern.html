🤖 4. MODERN TECHNIQUES

4.1 Deep Learning Approaches

Deep Learning helps recommenders discover hidden and complex patterns of users' habits. Models including Wide & Deep, Two-Tower Networks, and Neural Collaborative Filtering (NCF), capture complex non-linear relationships using neural networks. They improve generalization, handle high-dimensional features, and support large-scale industrial deployments.

4.2 Large Language Models.

Large Language Models (LLMs) like ChatGPT provide recommendations by understanding natural language, having conversations, and even explain why they recommend something. Together, they're transforming recommenders into AI assistants that understand your needs and have conversations with you.

Large Language Models (LLMs) advance recommenders by injecting rich semantic knowledge, understanding natural user preferences, and enabling generative, conversational, and open-world recommendation (Li et al., 2024; IEEE TKDE, 2024). They unify textual content understanding and user behavior modeling in order to support zero-shot adaptation and multi-task generalization (Bao et al., 2024).
